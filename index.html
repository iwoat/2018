International Workshop in Algebraic Topology

  • Southern University of Science and Technology
  • Shenzhen, China
  • June 6-9, 2018

Description

The International Workshop in Algebraic Topology is a four day event which will take place at the Southern University of Science and Technology in Shenzhen, China from June 6 to June 9, 2018. It is a satellite event for the AMS Special Session on Algebraic and Geometric Topology which will take place at Fudan University in Shanghai, China from June 11 to June 14, 2018.

Organizers

  • Agnès Beaudry, University of Colorado Boulder
  • Mark Behrens, University of Notre Dame
  • Zhen Huan, Sun Yat-sen University
  • Haynes Miller, Massachusetts Institute of Technology
  • Nathaniel Stapleton, University of Kentucky
  • Guozhen Wang, Shanghai Center for Mathematical Sciences
  • Zhouli Xu, Massachusetts Institute of Technology
  • Yifei Zhu, Southern University of Science and Technology
