Special Session on Algebraic and Geometric Topology

  • Fudan University
  • Shanghai, China

Description

This Special Session on Algebraic and Geometric Topology is part of the Joint International Meeting of the American Mathematical Society and the Chinese Mathematical Society which will take place at Fudan University in Shanghai, China from June 11 to June 14, 2018.

  • Zhi Lü, Fudan University

  • Yifei Zhu, Southern University of Science and Technology
